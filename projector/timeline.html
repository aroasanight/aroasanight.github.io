Projector Development Timeline

Step One: Light Source

A powerful light source emits a beam of intense, white light which passes through a light integrator rod, distributing light evenly.

Step Two: Color Separation

White light is split into red, green, and blue beams using dichroic mirrors, which reflect specific wavelengths of light.

Step Three: LCD Processing

The red, green, and blue beams pass through individual LCD screens, where liquid crystals manipulate light to form images in different brightnesses.

Step Four: Image Recombination

The colored images recombine in a dichroic prism to form a full-color image.

Step Five: Projection

The final image is projected through a lens and onto a screen, completing the projection process.
